WHO Partograph Guidelines
Normal Progress: ≥1 cm/hour after 4 cm dilation
Warning: <0.5 cm/hour after 4 cm dilation
Critical: Crossing Action Line (4 hours right of Alert Line)
  • Alert Line: 1 cm/hour from 4cm to 10cm (6 hours total)
  • Action Line: 4 hours to the right of Alert Line
  • Optimal contractions: 3-5 per 10 minutes
  • Normal station: -3 (floating) to +3 (crowning)
